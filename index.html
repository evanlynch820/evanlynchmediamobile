About Me

Note: This website is still under construction. I am adding mobile functionality and am currently adding more of my work.

I am a junior in high school based in Chattanooga with a passion for all things marketing related. I recently obtained a certification in Digital Marketing from Hubspot Academy, and I love working in both sports and childcare. I have been in photography for over 2 years now, and videography for about a year and a half. I currently work as a game-day photographer and videographer for the Chattanooga Lookouts, a Double-A baseball team. In June 2023, I began work as the social media manager for Camp Big Fish, a children's day-camp located mainly in East Tennessee, but expanding towards Middle Tennessee. I also work with Silverdale Baptist Academy to create content for the football and boys basketball teams. Services that I offer include digital marketing, photography, videography, social media management, and graphic design. In the future I will likely also offer web development.

Contact me for any inquiries about anything! Feel free to email me or message me on Instagram. Contact me at evan@evanlynchmedia.com, or @evanlynchmedia on Instagram.
